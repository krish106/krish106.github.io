Privacy Policy for Arion VPN

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Arion VPN. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. No Logs Policy

Arion VPN operates under a strict No-Logs Policy. We do not collect, store, or share any of the following data:

  • IP Addresses (Local or VPN-assigned)
  • Browsing History
  • Traffic Destination or Metadata
  • DNS Queries
  • Connection Timestamps or Session Durations

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or provide any personal identification to use the core features of the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App utilizes the public SoftEther and VPNGate volunteer network. While we do not log your data, volunteer servers are operated by third parties. By using the App, you acknowledge that you are routing your traffic through public infrastructure.

We use Google AdMob to provide advertisements that keep the App free. AdMob may collect certain device information (such as Advertising IDs) to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
